About Wikipedia Knowledge Map

This interactive demo maps your knowledge across 250,000 Wikipedia articles using text embedding models. Articles are projected into a 2D space where semantically related topics cluster together — physics near mathematics, neuroscience near biology.

As you answer questions, a Gaussian process estimates your knowledge across the entire domain, filling in a heatmap that reveals both your strengths and gaps. The system uses active learning to select the most informative questions, efficiently mapping your knowledge with as few questions as possible.

Because the map lives in embedding space, answering questions in one domain (e.g., Mathematics) produces predictions in related domains (e.g., Probability & Statistics) — demonstrating that text embeddings capture conceptual relationships.
